The Maryland Centers for Psychiatry offers specialized and general mental health services for children, adolescents, and adults at multiple locations in Central Maryland with current offices in Ellicott City and Annapolis. The Centers consist of multidisciplinary teams who evaluate a wide range of behavioral, learning, and emotional problems and then design a program of care emphasizing an integrated approach focusing on the uniqueness of each individual and family.

The staff of The Centers remain strongly committed to the belief that one cannot treat an individual in isolation. Rather, we believe that it is important to attend to all the elements of an individual's life including family, school system, and the work environment. Based on The Center's philosophy, treatment programs typically extend counseling and educational services beyond the identified patient (e.g. family, school).
